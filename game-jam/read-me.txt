this game is inteded for play with more than one player and up to 4 players

it can be played by pressing enter on keyboard or start on controller

survive as long as you can while sweeping away boxes with your broom

Keyboard
WASD - Move
E - Sweep Brush
Space - Jump

Controller
Left Stick - Move
Left Bumper - Sweep Brush
South Button (A or X) - Jump

hope you enjoy!
